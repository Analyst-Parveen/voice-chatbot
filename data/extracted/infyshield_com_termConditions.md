### 1. Legal Status and Nature of the Agreement

- **Contractual Definition:**This Protection Plan is strictly a- **service contract**administered by Infinity Assurance Solutions Private Limited; it is- **explicitly not an insurance policy.**

### 2. Plan Types & Strict Purchase Timelines

The validity of a customer's claim relies heavily on when they purchased the protection plan relative to their original product purchase date:

- **Extended Warranty (EW) Plan:**Covers sudden, unexpected electrical or mechanical breakdowns and component defects under normal operating conditions. This coverage begins the day after the original manufacturer’s warranty expires. It- **must be purchased within the first 9 months**of the product purchase date, provided the item has not suffered any prior damage, defect, or operational loss.
- **Accidental Damage Protection (ADP) Plan:**Covers sudden and unforeseen physical damage caused by external factors—such as accidental drops, structural impacts, or liquid spillages (including water, tea, or coffee)—under normal working conditions. This plan- **must be purchased on the same day**as the original product.
- **Screen Damage Protection (SDP) Plan:**Provides targeted coverage against sudden external impacts or accidental physical drops that damage the device's display screen. Crucially, this plan- **only covers the cost of the screen (display module) and associated labor.**If an accident damages internal components or external casings alongside the screen, the cost of repairing those non-screen parts- **must be paid entirely by the customer.**This plan- **must be purchased on the same day**as the original product.
- **Combo Protection Plan (CPP):**A bundled contract linking two or more individual coverage types (such as Extended Warranty + Accidental Damage Protection or Extended Warranty + Screen Damage Protection) under a single service plan. This combo plan- **must be purchased on the same day**as the original product.
