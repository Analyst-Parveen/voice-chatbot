## We are committed to protect your privacy

### We hereby state and explain our online information practices

During the course of our interactions with you including but not limited to while making enquiries, making purchases, amending purchases requests, product registration, taking service, making claims etc. you will be sharing certain information including your contact details like email addresses, contact number, addresses, product details, certain pointed information during our surveys, feedback sessions etc. This information may be collected through Web Forms on our websites, applications, emails, telephonic interactions, written documents etc.

The information that we collect may be received by us directly from you, our affiliates, partners in our business to deliver products & services to you, insurance agents, manufacturer / retailer of your products, service center, call center etc.

We hereby state our intention to use the information collected solely for the purpose of providing various products and services to you which might include sharing information with our employees who are responsible to provide certain services to you, our business partners, affiliates, outsourced agencies, principals etc. as required by law.

We do not sell your information to any third parties, and we do not disclose it to third parties that are not affiliated with us unless necessary to service your policy, process a claim or as otherwise permitted by law.

If you have any questions / concerns, please free to let us know at contact@infinityassurance.com
