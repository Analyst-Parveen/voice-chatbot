## Frequently Asked Questions

About Business Partnership

#### 1. All products have warranty from the manufacturer. Why should I then buy InfyShield?

You love your product and it is more likely to fail as it is used.

Electronics Hardware Technology is getting hugely integrated and complex. In out of warranty conditions, the cost of repairs (Labor Charges + Cost of Parts) are very high; many times it becomes unaffordable forcing you to scrap it and buy a new product. And in some cases, due to non-availability of certain parts with your manufacturer, you have no option but to buy a new product.

You may have also come across situations when a product malfunctions multiple times which not just irritates you but no one comes to the rescue; so you are forced to scrap the same.

InfyShield Service Plans provide protection against such high costs and prohibitive situations by extending the services with a low fixed cost. We don’t stop there: if due to any of the above reasons, there is a need to replace the product, we bear the cost. InfyShield Service Plans therefore get you Total Protection.

#### 2. Does InfyShield Extended Warranty Service Plan cover all parts of my product?

Yes, InfyShield Extended Warranty Service Plan covers all internal electrical and mechanical parts which are necessary for the product to function when you use your product with care and under your manufacturer’s operating guidelines & environment.

It is important for you to know that damages or defects caused by unauthorized repair, incorrect installation, theft, burglary, accident, negligence, intentional damage, abuse or misuse of the product, fire, use on an incorrect voltage, power dips, poor environmental operating conditions, household pets, rats, cockroaches or any other animals or insects, Force Majeure etc. are not covered.

The Extended Warranty Service plans also do not cover accessories, consumables, external items etc. e.g. external cables, toner, cartridges, batteries, chargers, adapters etc. Specific exclusions related to certain products may also apply.

You must read the terms and conditions of your Service Plan very carefully before requesting for service. Those services and parts which are not covered in part or full in the Service Plan are charged extra.

#### 3. What is the InfyShield Accidental Damage Protection Service Plan and what does it cover?

We all love our products and take care of them but there are unforeseen situations when despite due care, accidents happen. In many situations, the products get damaged.

Even during Warranty period, the damages due to an accident are not covered by the manufacturer of your product; and the repair costs are high. It may also happen the product may become irreparable or it is economically unviable to get the product repaired.

These **InfyShield Accidental Damage Protection Service Plans are Great Solution for you; especially for products that are portable e.g. Mobile Phones, Tablets, e-book Readers, Laptops, Cameras etc. and are a MUST have.**

As the Service Plan type suggests, InfyShield **Accidental Damage Protection (ADP) takes care of product malfunction when they are damaged due to an accident.**

**These plans also cover all internal** electrical and mechanical parts which are necessary for the product to function but the cause of their malfunction is an established condition of an accident.

It is important for you to know that damage or defects caused by unauthorized repair, incorrect installation, theft, burglary, terrorism, abuse or misuse of the product, negligence, intentional damage, fire, use on an incorrect voltage, power dips, poor environmental operating conditions, thunderstorm, household pets, rats, cockroaches or any other animals or insects, Force Majeure etc. are not covered. The plans also do not cover accessories, consumables, external items etc. e.g. external cables, toner, cartridges, batteries, chargers, adapters etc. Specific exclusions related to certain products may also apply. You must read the terms and conditions of your Service Plan very carefully before requesting for service. Those services and parts which are not covered in part or full in the Service Plan are charged extra. For any doubts or clarifications, please feel free to contact us.

#### 4. I noticed there is a InfyShield Screen Damage Protection (SDP) Service Plan also? What is that and how is it different from

A Great observation indeed. We have curated Service Plans suiting various needs of our esteemed customers.

The SDP covers the most failing parts in situations of the accident i.e. the Display / screen. You know that the cost of the Display or the screen is much higher than any other part (in many cases it is more than 2/3rd of the cost of the whole product). In general, the SDP covers the Display / screen only while ADP covers all other internal parts of the products (please refer to the answers under section ADP and also refer to the service plan details as there may be multiple options).

#### 5. What should I consider before I buy any InfyShield Service Plan?

This is an important question. Here’s what you should consider:

- Review whether you actually need a service plan e.g. you should check whether your product is covered under some other service plan or insurance.
- Short term service plans are costlier. For example, a one year extended service plan and renewing it for another year will be costlier than directly buying a 2 years Extended Warranty Service Plan.
- Buy the service plans at the first available opportunity. The Accidental Damage Protection plan cannot be purchased at a later date and buying the Extended Warranty Plan at the time of product purchase will be cheaper than buying the same towards the end of the standard warranty period.
- Look at the profile of the promoters of the warranty provider. Professionals in the customer service industry will be most suited to understand your service needs. Don’t fall for the wrong warranty service provider.
- Be careful and read all inclusions and exclusions i.e. what is covered and what is not covered under your selected InfyShield Service Plan.

If you have any questions or concerns, you must share them with us. You should buy the Service Plans only after assuring yourself on your purchase.

#### 6. When can I buy InfyShield Service Plans?

All Extended Warranty Service Plans can be purchased only within a specified limit, normally within 6 months from the date of purchase of your product.

All Accidental Damage Protection or Screen Damage Protection Plans or any plans which have such coverage can be bought only on the same day of product purchase; and must not be damaged or defective at such time.

We recommend you to buy InfyShield at the very first available opportunity i.e. at the time of purchasing your product itself to save costs.

#### 7. When do I become eligible to avail service under InfyShield Service Plans?

The whole objective of InfyShield Service Plans is to provide uninterrupted services to your product in case it develops a defect or is accidentally damaged.

Services under InfyShield Extended Warranty Service Plan commence from the very next day when your product manufacturer’s warranty expires.

Services under Accidental Damage Protection / Screen Damage Protection Plan commences in 7-15 days (please check the terms at the time of buying the service plan) from the date of purchase of your product unless your product is already covered for Accidental Protection by the manufacturer. In such a case the Accidental Damage Protection Plan shall also start from the very next day the manufacturer’s coverage expires.

#### 8. How to avail service?

It is simple but before you panic and call us, we recommend you to check that the malfunctioning of your product is not due to external sources like network signals, incorrect settings, internet connectivity, improper power supply etc. This will help you resolve the problem quickly.

If your equipment still malfunctions, please contact our Service Desk as per details mentioned in the Contact Us section of our website or on the InfyShield Plan you have purchased.

If your equipment is damaged due to accidental damage, please contact our Service Desk.

A small service order charge or deductible is due to be paid in certain Service Plans.

After receiving your service request, we will respond quickly to solve the problem through our call centre. If the problem is not solved, we will depute our technician to visit you (in case of On-site Service Plan) or you will have to take your product along with a copy of proof of this plan to the nearest authorized service center (in case of Carry-in Service Plan) or arrange a pickup as it may be applicable.

Please Note: Do not carry your product directly to any service centre whether or not authorized by us without prior approval of our Service Desk. This is to ensure you get services to the best of our efforts and updated contacts list.

Yes, registration is mandatory for us to know your product description, product purchase details, your contact details, location, warranty start & end dates etc. This ensures that we will be able to plan and equip ourselves to deliver you excellent service as and when you need it.

You should register your service plan at the very first available opportunity which may be at the time of purchase of InfyShield and you must register within 3 business days of purchase of InfyShield to get uninterrupted services. The onus of registerting your products with all documents and complete & correct information lies solely with you. We are not obligated to provide services or make a refund to the products whose InfyShield Service Plan is NOT registered with us within specified timelines under any conditions.

#### 13. How many services can I get during the service plan period?

We recommend you going through your InfyShield Service Plan as these vary. In some of the plans, you may avail multiple services while some are limited with numbers and / or by the value.

#### 14. What will happen if your Authorised Service Centre is not able to repair my product?

This is an unlikely situation because services are normally rendered by Brand Authorised / Technically competent Service Centres only. However, in such a situation, we will replace your product with an equivalent model with similar or better specification of same or similar make at our own discretion as per terms and conditions of the Service Plan document, whose value cannot be more than “the original product purchase price or the prevailing market price of such a product” minus the cost of services already delivered; the depreciation & salvage value will apply as per your service plan.

#### 15. I have bought a product from outside India. Can I get my product covered under InfyShield's Service Plans?

We are thankful for your faith in InfyShield but regret to inform you that InfyShield Service Plans can be purchased for products purchased in India only and those which have manufacturer’s warranty coverage available in India. However, please leave the description of your product including make, model, serial number, product condition, your contact details etc. in our Contact Form. We will contact you once we have a solution for you.
